Mikko Lepistö, known as Drawpistol, lives and breathes freestyle. He specializes in creative flows where nothing is pre-planned, with everything born spontaneously from the moment’s feeling. His art is like a continuous game where chance and skill intertwine seamlessly. The pandemic led him to discover a completely unique technique that brings his improvisation to a new level. Therefore each work of his is a manifestation of freedom and self-expression.
